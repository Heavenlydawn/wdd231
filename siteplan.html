Site Name

Site Purpose

GlowFinder provides a user-friendly platform where skincare enthusiasts and beginners can explore detailed information about natural skincare ingredients, their benefits, and recommended usage. It offers personalized skincare tips and ingredient tracking to help users build effective skincare routines.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: "I have sensitive skin and want to find natural ingredients that are gentle and safe for my skin type. How can I identify these ingredients?"
  2. Scenario 2: "I want to track which skincare ingredients I have tried and monitor their effects. Can I easily log and review this information?"
